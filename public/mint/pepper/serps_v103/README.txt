PEPPER MAKES MINT BETTER


Installing Pepper
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Upload the /serps/ directory and its contents to /mint/pepper/ravenseo/.
   If the directory /ravenseo/ doesn't exist, create it.

2. Login to your Mint installation and in the Preferences click
   "Install" under Pepper.

3. Click the SERPs Pepper "Install" button. Click "Okay".


Updating Pepper
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Delete the /serps/ directory on the server.

2. Upload the /serps/ directory and its contents to /mint/pepper/ravenseo/.
