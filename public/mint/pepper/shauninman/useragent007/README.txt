PEPPER MAKES MINT BETTER

Installing Pepper
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Upload the /useragent007/ directory and its contents to 
/mint/pepper/shauninman/. If the directory /shauninman/ doesn't exist, create it.

2. Login to your Mint installation and in the Preferences click "Install" under
Pepper.

3. Click the User Agent 007 Pepper "Install" button. Click "Okay."
