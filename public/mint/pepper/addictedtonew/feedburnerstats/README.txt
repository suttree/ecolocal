ADDICTED TO FEEDBURNER

Installing Pepper
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Upload the /feedburner/ directory and its contents to 
/mint/pepper/addictedtonew/. If the directory /addictedtonew/ doesn't exist, create it.

2. Login to your Mint installation and in the Preferences click "Install" under
Pepper.

3. Click the Addicted To Feedburner "Install" button. Click "Okay."
